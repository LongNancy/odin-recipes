Macca

Macaroni and cheese can be as easy or involved as you want to make it.

Macaroni and cheese is a staple in any diet as it provides carbs and dairy as well as protein when chicken or hot dogs are added. Pork or beef hot dogs make a great addition to any stovetop macaroni and cheese.

You'll also see in this recipe that it uses a roux base. Making a roux is a basic kitchen skill that can be applied in a wide variety of recipes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Milk
  • 4 Tbls Flour
  • 4 Tbls Butter

  • Salt, Pepper, Italian Seasoning
  • 2 cups Sharp Cheddar, grated
  • 3 Hot Dogs, chopped

  • 4 cups Cooked Elbow Noodled

Steps

  1. Make the roux by cooking the butter and flour together until fragrant and then add the milk one dollop at a time mixing the whole time until all is combined without clumps.
  2. Add the cheese and let it melt into the sauce.
  3. Add the hot dogs and let it stew for a couple of minutes on low to help spread the flavor.
  4. Add spices to your taste before adding the cooked macaroni.

*It should be noted that you should always cook your pasta in salted water. It should be as briney as the sea!

**It should also be noted that I cook this dish by eyeing the ingredients as I've been making it for years. The amounts listed here are arbitrary but in the ballpark of correct.
