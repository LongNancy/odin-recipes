Ice Tea

Macaroni and cheese can be as easy or involved as you want to make it.

Macaroni and cheese is a staple in any diet as it provides carbs and dairy as well as protein when chicken or hot dogs are added. Pork or beef hot dogs make a great addition to any stovetop macaroni and cheese.

You'll also see in this recipe that it uses a roux base. Making a roux is a basic kitchen skill that can be applied in a wide variety of recipes.

Ingredients

  • 2 qt water
  • 3/4 cup white sugar

  • 1 family sized black tea bag
  • 3 regular sized strawberry hibiscus tea bags

Steps

  1. Boil the water on the stove
  2. Add the teabags and let sit for 15-20 minutes
  3. Remove the teabags and add the sugar, stirring until dissolved
  4. Let cool on the stove before putting into a pitcer

*Best enjoyed in a tall glass full of ice.
